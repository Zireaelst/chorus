# Chorus Contributor License Agreement (Reference Only)

> [!WARNING]  
> **THIS IS NOT A FINAL OR ENFORCEABLE LEGAL DOCUMENT.**  
> This file serves solely as a reference point for the legal reviewer finalizing the terms ahead of the v0.8 open-source transition (as noted in `CONTRIBUTING.md`). 

## Reference Template
We are proposing to base the Chorus CLA on the standard **Apache Individual Contributor License Agreement (ICLA)**.

A generic template of the Apache ICLA can be found at:  
[https://www.apache.org/licenses/icla.pdf](https://www.apache.org/licenses/icla.pdf)

## Review Required
Legal review is required to:
1. Confirm whether the standard Apache ICLA meets the liability and copyright assignment requirements for Chorus.
2. Determine if a Corporate CLA (CCLA) is also required for entity contributors.
3. Finalize the exact legal text to be presented to contributors in the public repository.
